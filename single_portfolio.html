Procedures

Dr. Kim practices both surgical and non-surgical microscopic-enhanced endodontics. He is proud to carry state-of-the-art technologies such as GentleWave® and BIOLASE laser treatments.

Teaching

Dr. Kim is an adjunct instructor in the Department of Endodontics at Columbia University College of Dental Medicine. He also serves on the Resident Expert Advisory Council in the Foundation for Endodontics.

Commitment

Dr. Kim brings a wealth of expertise and dedication to every patient he treats. With a focus on high-level precision care, he strives to deliver the best experience possible. He is deeply aligned with the practice’s mission, making a positive impact on the local dental community.

A Journey of Dedication

Dr. Lee was born in Seoul and moved to San Francisco as a teenager. He arrived in the U.S. speaking limited English but excelled academically, discovering his passion for dentistry early on. His experiences inspired his commitment to providing compassionate care and excellence in endodontics.

Committed to Excellence in Endodontics

Driven by a commitment to innovation and continuous improvement, Dr. Lee strives to offer the most advanced, patient-focused endodontic care available. His approach combines precision, cutting-edge technology, and a focus on patient comfort.

Beyond the Practice

Outside of his work, Dr. Lee enjoys playing chess, collecting fine watches, exploring Northern Virginia, and cherishing time with his wife, Katherine, and their daughter, Yui.
